My Story

Hi, I'm a product designer with a sharp eye for color harmony, shape relationships, and visual storytelling. I believe good design feels natural, and I enjoy the process just as much as the outcome. Beyond the screen, you'll find me solving sudoku puzzles, dancing to a beat, or striking a pose for the camera. Having fun off-screen helps me stay creative on-screen. Design is my craft, play is my fuel, and I'm always chasing the next bold idea and making it happen.

From designing life-changing products to leading competitive engineering teams, I believe in the power of thoughtful design to create meaningful impact. Every project is an opportunity to solve real problems and make the world a little bit better.
