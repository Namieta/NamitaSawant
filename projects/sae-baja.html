Technical Approach

My technical work focused on creating a robust, lightweight chassis that met strict safety requirements while optimizing for performance and manufacturability.

Chassis Design Process

Started with ergonomic analysis for driver accommodation, then developed space frame geometry using CAD modeling. Ensured compliance with SAE Baja India rulebook requirements including impact zones, roll cage specifications, and material constraints.

FEA Methodology

Performed static and dynamic analysis using industry-standard software. Evaluated multiple load cases including frontal impact, side impact, torsional loads, and operational stresses. Validated safety factors and identified optimization opportunities.

Design Optimization

Iteratively refined tube sizing, joint design, and overall geometry based on FEA results. Balanced weight reduction with safety requirements, achieving significant mass savings without compromising structural performance.

Business Development
