Competition Overview

SAE Baja India challenges undergraduate students to design, build, test, and race a single-seat, off-road vehicle. Teams are evaluated on design innovation, cost optimization, manufacturing feasibility, and dynamic performance across multiple events including acceleration, maneuverability, suspension & traction, and a grueling 4-hour endurance race.
